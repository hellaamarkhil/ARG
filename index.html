About Cooklet

Welcome to Cooklet — your personal, digital recipe book made for modern kitchens and curious cooks. Whether you’re saving grandma’s secret sauce, jotting down a late-night experiment, or bookmarking your go-to weeknight dinners, Cooklet makes it effortless to collect, organize, and revisit your favorite flavors. Built for food lovers of all kinds, Cooklet turns messy notes and scattered screenshots into a beautifully organized home for your recipes. With smart tools and a minimalist design, it’s cooking made simple and delicious.

Recipe of the Day "Maqluba"

Maqluba is a traditional Middle Eastern dish that’s as stunning as it is satisfying. Meaning “upside down” in Arabic, this one-pot wonder features layers of spiced rice, tender meat, and golden fried vegetables, all cooked together and flipped to reveal a beautiful, stacked masterpiece. Comforting, aromatic, and perfect for gatherings, Maqluba is both a meal and a moment.
